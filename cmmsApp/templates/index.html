Electrical & Energy Audit Services

We support industrial, commercial and infrastructure clients with audits that improve safety, compliance, reliability and operational efficiency.

Electrical Safety Audit

Review of electrical installations to identify unsafe conditions, hazards and deviations from applicable safety requirements.

Scope

Inspection of switchboards, wiring systems, protection arrangements, installation condition and site electrical practices.

Outputs

  • Safety findings and risk observations
  • Non-compliance register
  • Corrective action recommendations

Energy Audit and Efficiency Review

Assessment of energy usage, operating patterns and equipment performance to identify losses and savings opportunities.

Scope

Load review, operating profile analysis, equipment efficiency checks and identification of energy-saving opportunities.

Outputs

  • Energy performance observations
  • Improvement opportunities
  • Recommendations to reduce operational costs

Compliance Assessment

Audit support aligned with applicable regulations and referenced standards for safer and legally compliant operations.

Scope

Review against the Work Health and Safety Regulation 2011 and referenced standards such as AS3000, AS3100 and AS61502.

Outputs

  • Compliance gap identification
  • Priority action items
  • Support for rectification planning

Scope

Outputs

  • Support for safer system operation

Why it matters

Why Regular Electrical and Energy Audits Are Important

Electrical non-compliance and inefficient operation can lead to safety incidents, equipment failures, business interruption, excess energy costs and avoidable maintenance issues.

A structured audit helps identify hidden risks early, prioritize corrective work and create a safer and more efficient operating environment.

  1. Inspect

    Review installations, equipment condition, operating risks and energy performance on site.

  2. Identify

    Highlight unsafe conditions, non-compliance, inefficiencies and priority risk areas.

  3. Report

    Deliver a clear audit report with findings, observations and recommended actions.

  4. Improve

    Implement practical corrective measures to improve safety, compliance and efficiency.
